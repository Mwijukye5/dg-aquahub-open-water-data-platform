# DG AquaHub – Open Water Data Platform
> **Developed by Drought Guard Africa**  
> Smart, solar-powered water infrastructure for climate-vulnerable communities across Africa.

---

## Overview

DG AquaHub is an open, data-driven platform that enables real-time monitoring and intelligent optimization of solar-powered water kiosks and irrigation systems in climate-vulnerable communities.

The platform integrates IoT sensing, AI analytics, and flexible payment infrastructure — including mobile money and prepaid smart card (tap-to-dispense) systems — to improve water access, system performance, and service reliability for underserved populations.

Community operators, particularly women and youth, are trained to manage systems locally — ensuring long-term sustainability and community ownership.

---

## The Problem

Millions of people across Africa lack reliable access to safe water:

- Children face waterborne diseases, poor hygiene, and malnutrition
- Girls miss school collecting water for hours every day
- Existing systems are manual, fragmented, and have no real-time visibility
- Climate change is making droughts longer and water sources less predictable

Without data-driven infrastructure, communities cannot plan, adapt, or respond.

---

## The Solution

DG AquaHub delivers a layered digital and physical water infrastructure:

- **Real-time monitoring** of usage, flow rates, pressure, and payments
- **AI-driven analytics** for demand forecasting and fault detection
- **Dual payment systems** — mobile money and prepaid smart cards (tap to dispense)
- **Community-managed operations** — women and youth trained as certified AquaHub operators
- **Open dashboards** for operators, governments, NGOs, and development partners

---

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| IoT Data Collection | Flow meters, pressure gauges, quality probes, usage counters |
| Real-Time Dashboards | Web and mobile interfaces for live monitoring and reporting |
| AI Demand Forecasting | Predictive models to optimize supply and flag anomalies early |
| Offline-First Architecture | Full functionality in low-connectivity environments, syncs on reconnect |
| Mobile Money Integration | M-Pesa, MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, and equivalents |
| Prepaid Smart Card System | Tap-to-dispense card payments for communities with limited phone access |
| Open Data Architecture | Interoperable APIs for third-party and government system integration |
| Community Operator Tools | Tablet-based management tools for trained local operators |

## Open Source Commitment

This repository represents the open digital layer of DG AquaHub. We are committed to making core components accessible to governments, NGOs, researchers, and development partners — including:

- Data schemas and API specifications
- Dashboard modules
- Analytical models and forecasting tools

Our goal is to enable replication and scaling of climate-resilient water solutions globally, without reinventing infrastructure from scratch. Contributions and partnerships are welcome.
