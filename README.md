
# DG AquaHub – Open Water Data Platform

## Overview
DG AquaHub is an open, data-driven platform developed by Drought Guard Africa to support real-time monitoring and optimization of automated, solar-powered water kiosks and irrigation systems in climate-vulnerable communities.

The platform enables tracking of water access, system performance, and usage patterns to improve service delivery, strengthen climate resilience, and enhance child health outcomes.

---

## Problem
Millions of people in underserved communities lack reliable access to safe water, exposing children to waterborne diseases, poor hygiene, and malnutrition. Existing water systems are often manual, fragmented, and lack real-time data for planning and response.

---

## Solution
DG AquaHub provides:
- Real-time monitoring of water usage, payments, and system performance
- AI-driven analytics for demand prediction and system optimization
- Data dashboards for operators, governments, and partners
- Integration with mobile money systems for seamless payments

---

## Key Features
- IoT-enabled data collection (flow, pressure, usage)
- Real-time dashboards for system monitoring
- AI-based demand forecasting and fault detection
- Offline-first functionality for low-connectivity environments
- Open and interoperable data architecture

---

## Use Cases
- Community water kiosks in rural and refugee settings
- Health facilities and schools requiring reliable water access
- Government planning and early warning systems
- Climate resilience and water resource management

---

## Impact
- Improves access to safe water for children and vulnerable populations
- Reduces waterborne diseases and improves hygiene outcomes
- Supports food security through water access and irrigation
- Enables data-driven decision-making at scale

---

## Open Source Commitment
This repository represents the open digital layer of DG AquaHub. We are committed to making core components of our data systems accessible to governments, NGOs, and partners to support replication and scaling of climate-resilient water solutions globally.

---

## Architecture (Sample)
- IoT Sensors → Data Collection Layer  
- Data Processing → Cloud / Edge Systems  
- AI Analytics → Demand Prediction & Optimization  
- Dashboard Interface → Web & Mobile Access
